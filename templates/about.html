Why NEPHRO Expert?

The GFR Equation Mismatch

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) affects millions in Saudi Arabia, with most cases diagnosed at late stages.
Accurate GFR measurement is crucial for early detection and proper management.
But not all GFR equations are equal — using the wrong one can cause serious medication dosing errors.
One study showed that 50% of patients were misdosed due to equation mismatch, leading to harmful outcomes.

Our Mission

To address the discrepancies between GFR equations in CKD care and introduce a targeted solution that enhances dosing accuracy,
reduces patient harm, and supports clinical decision-making.

How NEPHRO Expert Helps

  • Smart Equation Selector: Automatically selects the most suitable GFR equation based on patient data — no guessing, more accuracy.
  • Drug Dosing Recommendations: Adjusts medication doses based on kidney function, reducing risks of overdose or toxicity.
  • Renal Tracker & Predictor: Tracks GFR over time and predicts kidney decline early — enabling timely intervention.
